# Prompt for AI Designer: Main Hero Logo `LAL.PRO × Avito`

## Task
Create the main combined logo/signature for the landing page hero section.
The logo must visually combine **LAL.PRO** and **Avito** into one clean, premium, modern composition.
This is not a random decorative illustration — it is the **main brand lockup for the first screen** of the landing page.

## Context
We are building a landing page for Avito Ads in a clean editorial/minimalist style.
The page uses a lot of white space, black typography, строгая сетка, аккуратные рамки, and premium minimal composition.
The combined logo will be used in the hero section and must feel like a collaboration mark:

`LAL.PRO × Avito`

I will provide:
- the original LAL.PRO logo
- the Avito logo/reference
- optional photo/reference files
- screenshots of the landing page hero section

Use those materials as the visual base.

## Main Objective
Design a **hero-ready combined logo** that:
- clearly shows both brands
- feels premium and modern
- fits the minimalist landing page design
- looks clean both on white and light-gray backgrounds
- can be used in website hero, presentations, and brand materials

## Design Direction
Follow this style:
- minimalist
- modern
- premium
- editorial
- clean geometry
- strong typography
- no clutter
- no childish effects
- no random 3D decorations
- no stickers, emojis, or playful internet-style elements

The result should feel like a serious partnership mark for a premium marketing/advertising landing page.

## Required Composition
Create a combined lockup using:
- the **LAL.PRO** logo
- the **Avito** logo or logotype
- a separator between them, preferably `×` or another elegant collaboration marker

Possible directions:
1. Horizontal lockup:
   `LAL.PRO × Avito`
2. Balanced co-branding mark with equal visual weight
3. Version with logo symbols + version with wordmarks only

## Visual Requirements
- Keep brand recognition of both sides
- Do not distort the logos beyond recognition
- Maintain clean spacing and optical balance
- Make it visually strong enough for hero usage
- It must work at medium and large sizes
- It must remain readable on desktop and mobile
- The design must look good centered inside a hero image area

## Color Requirements
Primary approach:
- black / deep charcoal
- white
- subtle use of Avito brand colors only if it strengthens recognition

Important:

- do not overload with gradients
- avoid bright promotional/banner style
- keep the result elegant and restrained

## Background Compatibility
Prepare the concept so it works on:
- white background
- light gray background

## Deliverables
